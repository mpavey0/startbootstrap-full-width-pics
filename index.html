What is interpreteer?

volunteer | interpret | become an interpreteer

Refugees, or newcomers to Germany, often have difficulty understanding German, yet must communicate with German authorities from day one. Many Germans have language skills in Arabic, Somali, French or other languages and are willing to volunteer their time to help newcomers. We call these people interpreteers.

Interpreteer fosters intercultural understanding by overcoming language barriers. Enter your details above to be notified when we launch.
